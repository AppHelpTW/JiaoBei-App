## 隱私權政策 / Privacy Policy
**Jiao Bei** 非常重視您的個人隱私。  
**Jiao Bei** takes your personal privacy very seriously.

### 1. 資料收集 / Data Collection
我們**不會收集**、存取、儲存或分享使用者的任何個人識別資料（如姓名、地址、電話、Email 等）。  
We **do not collect**, access, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as name, address, phone number, email, etc.) from our users.

### 2. 裝置權限使用 / Device Permissions
* **加速規 (Accelerometer)**: 本 App 僅存取您的裝置感應器，用來感應您的搖動動作以觸發擲筊功能。這些數據僅在您的裝置上進行即時運算，絕不會被上傳或儲存。  
  The app accesses your device's accelerometer to detect shaking motions and trigger the divination function. This data is processed locally in real-time and is never uploaded or stored.
* **震動回饋 (Haptics)**: 僅用於提供模擬擲筊落地的體感回饋。  
  Used solely to provide haptic feedback simulating the impact of the Jiao Bei hitting the floor.

### 3. 第三方服務 / Third-Party Services
本 App 不包含任何第三方廣告追蹤或數據分析工具。  
This app does not contain any third-party advertising tracking or data analysis tools.

### 4. 政策修訂 / Policy Changes
若隱私權政策有任何變動，我們將更新此頁面。  
We will update this page if there are any changes to our privacy policy.
